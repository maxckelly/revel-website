About Us

Founded in Janurary of 2016 and based in Melbourne, Victoria, Australia. Revel is the first one-stop-shop platform that allows event hosts to discover, book and pay all the necessary event services for your event.

Whether you're a planning an 18th, 21st, Wedding, corporate event, festival or simply a gathering Revel makes it easier to discover bands, photographers, caterers, outdoor cinemas, sporting talent, speakers and more.
